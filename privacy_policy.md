# Privacy Policy for CogniFocus

Last updated: 2026

CogniFocus is a productivity application developed by CogniElevate to help users focus on tasks and manage work sessions.

## Information We Collect

CogniFocus may collect limited anonymous usage information through analytics services to help improve the app. This may include:

* App usage statistics (such as focus sessions started or completed)
* Device information (such as device type, operating system version)
* App performance and crash reports
* General interaction data (such as session duration)

This information is collected in an aggregated and anonymized manner and is used solely to improve the application.

## Analytics

CogniFocus uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, to understand how users interact with the app and to improve performance and usability.

Firebase Analytics may collect certain technical information such as device identifiers, usage patterns, and diagnostic data. This information does not personally identify individual users.

For more information, please review Google's privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Storage

Focus session data and productivity information created within the app are stored locally on the user's device.

## Data Sharing

CogniFocus does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

CogniFocus is not directed at children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.
