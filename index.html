About CogniFocus

CogniFocus is a productivity application designed to help users focus on tasks and manage work sessions efficiently.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is handled by the application, how it may be used, and the measures we take to protect user information.

🔍

Information Collection and Use

CogniFocus does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or precise location data.

The application may collect anonymous usage data to help improve functionality, performance, and user experience.

This may include:

• App usage statistics (e.g., focus sessions started or completed)
• Device information (device model, OS version)
• General interaction events within the application

📊

Analytics

CogniFocus uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, to better understand how users interact with the application.

Firebase Analytics may collect anonymous technical information such as device identifiers, app interaction data, and general usage patterns. This information helps improve app stability, usability, and performance.

For more information on how Google handles data, please review:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

💾

Local Data Storage

Focus session data and productivity activity created within CogniFocus are stored locally on the user's device and are not transmitted to external servers controlled by the developer.

🔒

Data Security

We value user privacy and take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information related to the application.

Because most data remains stored locally on the device, personal information is not exposed to external systems controlled by CogniFocus.

👶

Children's Privacy

CogniFocus is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the application, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or app functionality.

Any updates will be posted on this page along with the updated revision date.
