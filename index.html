About CogniFocus

CogniFocus is a productivity application designed to help users focus on tasks and manage work sessions efficiently.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we handle, how it is stored, and the measures we take to keep it safe.

🔍

Information Collection and Use

CogniFocus does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or location data.

The app may collect anonymous usage data — such as session activity or app performance information — solely to help improve the application and provide a better user experience.

💾

Local Data Storage

Any timer sessions or productivity data created within the app are stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to external servers.

🔗

Third-Party Services

CogniFocus may use third-party services such as analytics or crash reporting tools to improve stability and performance. These services may collect anonymous technical data such as device type or app usage statistics.

We only partner with services that adhere to responsible data practices and do not use this data to identify individual users.

🔒

Data Security

We value your privacy and take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect any information associated with the app. Because most data remains on-device, your personal information is never exposed to our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Updates will be posted on this page along with a revised "Last updated" date.
